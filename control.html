Ways to Reduce Pollution

  1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Minimize waste by reusing materials and recycling whenever possible.
  2. Adopt Renewable Energy: Use solar, wind, and other renewable energy sources to reduce carbon footprints.
  3. Plant More Trees: Trees absorb carbon dioxide and improve air quality.
  4. Implement Strict Laws: Enforce environmental laws to regulate emissions and waste disposal.

Control Measures Table

Pollution Type Control Measures
Air Pollution Use public transport, regulate industrial emissions, adopt clean energy sources.
Water Pollution Proper sewage treatment, ban plastic waste, regulate industrial discharges.
Soil Pollution Reduce pesticide use, recycle waste, promote afforestation.
Noise Pollution Use noise barriers, promote quiet zones, regulate construction activities.
